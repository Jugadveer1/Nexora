Our Story

From a hackathon idea to a revolutionary platform

The Beginning

It all started in the bustling corridors of Chitkara University, where four passionate computer science students came together with a shared vision. During a late-night coding session, we realized that brilliant startup ideas were dying not because they lacked potential, but because they couldn't access the right funding and mentorship.

As students ourselves, we witnessed countless innovative projects from our peers that never saw the light of day. Traditional funding was too complex, too slow, and too exclusive. We knew there had to be a better way.

"What if we could democratize startup funding using blockchain technology and AI mentorship?" - This question sparked the birth of Nexora.

2024 - The Hackathon

Four students from Chitkara University participated in a blockchain hackathon with a revolutionary idea: combining AI mentorship with decentralized funding. What started as a 48-hour coding marathon became the foundation of Nexora.

Development Phase

Fueled by the positive response from the hackathon, our team worked tirelessly to build a comprehensive platform. We integrated MetaMask for seamless crypto transactions, implemented AI-powered mentorship using advanced language models, and created an intuitive interface for both entrepreneurs and investors.

Platform Launch

After months of development, testing, and refinement, Nexora was born. Our platform now features project showcasing, crypto-based funding, AI mentorship, real-time analytics, and a thriving community of entrepreneurs and investors.

The Future

Today, Nexora stands as a testament to what passionate students can achieve. We're not just building a platform; we're creating an ecosystem where the next generation of entrepreneurs can thrive, regardless of their background or location.
